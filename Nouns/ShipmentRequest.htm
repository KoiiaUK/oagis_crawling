Element: ShipmentRequest

Name ShipmentRequest
Type ShipmentRequestType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation A Shipment Request is an instruction to arrange the shipment of product by a logistics provider and deliver the item instances from point A to point B. A ShipmentRequest will either result in a CarrierRoute (planned)or an Advance Shipment Notice.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
